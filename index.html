Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about livestock coverage, claims, and premiums.

What animals can be covered under AjahFi protect?

AjahFi currently focuses heavily on goat insurance (Pashu Suraksha) for smallholder households. We support multiple breeds like Barbari, Beetal, Jamnapari, and other indigenous breeds.

How is livestock verified during enrollment?

When you register through the AjahFi app, a certified field partner tags the animal with a secure ear tag. You then upload the tag photos and health certificates directly through the app to activate your coverage.

What causes of death are covered?

Our comprehensive plan covers death due to accidental injuries, specific diseases (e.g., PPR, Anthrax, Foot-and-mouth disease), and extreme weather conditions or natural calamities.

How do I submit an insurance claim?

You can lodge claims instantly using the AjahFi Mobile App. Open the 'File a Claim' tab, scan the ear tag with the app camera overlay, upload the death report or medical certificate, and submit. Settlements are sent directly to your linked bank account.

Privacy Policy

At AjahFi, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data and livestock records. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following details to facilitate our services:

  • Farmer Profile: Name, phone number, village location, SHG affiliations, and linked bank account information for claim payouts.
  • Livestock Records: Breed, age, physical dimensions, location tags, health certificates, and unique ear tag identification photos.
  • Claims Data: Photo evidence of animal ear tags, veterinary certification documents, and geo-location details at the time of claim filing.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your data to verify ownership, calculate fair premiums, process claims, prevent fraud, and deliver payouts directly to your linked bank account.

1. Enrollment and Eligibility

Farmers or SHG members must register valid livestock details. All registered goats must be tagged with a secure ear tag provided by certified AjahFi partners. Incorrect details or missing physical tags will invalidate the policy coverage.

2. Premium and Policy Period

Policies are activated upon successful premium payment. Each policy is valid for the specified term (e.g., 1 year) and covers death resulting from accidents, illness, and natural calamities.

3. Claim Requirements

In the event of animal death, claims must be filed within 24 hours. The claim filing process requires scanning the unique ear tag through the AjahFi app camera overlay and submitting the vet-certified death certificate. False declarations or tamper marks on the ear tag will result in immediate denial of claims and policy termination.

4. Premium Refund Policy

Paid premiums are non-refundable once the policy goes active. Claims are settled directly to the farmer's linked bank account upon verification by our regional claims unit.
